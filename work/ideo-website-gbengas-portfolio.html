Background
IDEO's website has been in operation for 7 years and has faced reduced conversion recently. This has raised the concern of senior staff and they requested a UX audit be done in order for us to identify where we were losing customers and suggest improvements. IDEO.
Problem
IDEO is looking to hire their first Senior UX designer to work on the website, they are looking for someone with experience managing teams and who doesn't need to be micromanaged. The person brought in is to work with the Global Brand Marketing team and come up with strategy to increase conversion.
‍
Solution
IDEO recruited me to work in this role with the Global Brand Marketing team and I was able to do a UX audit on the site within the first week of hire. Using Google Analytics I was able to identify pages with high drop off rates and ineffective sales funnels. After doing  analysis on the cognitive biases and principles that affect the UX of our website, we identified that the user are experiencing Hick's. law on some pages and the congnitive load required to click the CTA was burdensome.

We resulted in 2 solution, a long term solution and a short term solution. The long term solution was to redesign the entire website to have a consistent theme with the brand message "This Work Can't Wait". The short term solution was to redesign some pages and CTAs on the website to improve conversion.
