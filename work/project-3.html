Jamestown Coffee House App

Mobile App Design
Project Overview
Jamestown Coffee House is a cafe located in Osu, Accra and it is known for serving signature home-brewed coffees. Here you can experience local and international food, a great assortment of cocktails and live music daily. They are looking to expand the efficiency of their service by building an app that delivers coffee to their local customers using GPS. They also want to include a gifting feature as they launch the campaign. In this project my major contribution was high-fidelity prototypes and interaction design, Michael Sarpong took care of userflows and low-fidelity prototypes
