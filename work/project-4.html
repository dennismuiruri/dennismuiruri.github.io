Background
Heny is an iOS and Android app that enables people find authentic experiences. From kayaking to mountain climbing experiences, users can book an experience and invite. their friends to join the experience. This lifestyle app is best for group outings or office team bonding activities
Problem
In Ghana, there is no platform that handles the seamless end to end curation and delivery of authentic leisure experiences that customers are truly satisfied with. Curators also have a difficult time verifying attendees of this experiences and this often causes loss in revenue.

I tested using [maze.co](http://maze.co) to gather user feedback and discover pain points in the current design. I had 20 participants go through the mobile high-fidelity prototype to determine the usability of navigation, flow, information architecture, and design. I also observed and analyzed the user's interactions through remote video call, video recording, and note taking. With the help of the marketing manager, Lawrence, we recruited participants for  the curator and user flow. Overall, we received good feedback from the participants.
